WARLI ART

Warli art contains the form of lifestyle representation. All you see in warli painting is the reflection of their day-to-day lives. There are specific patterns & use of basic graphic vocabulary like a triangle, circles, dots, dashes, etc. And all of this together forms the unique storytelling of warli art. Tribal artists narrate the simplicity through their artwork. Tarpa dance is the most central aspect of warli paintings. Warli tribes & artists always present the togetherness & uniqueness of their community. Warli art is a vivid expression of daily & special events of tribal communities, the warli culture is centered around the concept of nature & wildlife.

GOND ART

Gond art is originated by Gond Tribes in Madhya Pradesh. This popular art among tribal communities is based on their ancient myths, natural objects & folklore. Gond artists not only rely on what they have got in heritage but they also go beyond their memories & imagination to create the paintings.
